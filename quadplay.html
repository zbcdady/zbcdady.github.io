Why QuadPlay

Built for comparison, not clutter.

When you are reviewing edits, checking timing, or studying reference footage, the work is not about a giant media library. It is about keeping the right clips visible, responsive, and easy to control. QuadPlay keeps the interface focused on that job.

01

Side-by-side review

Open multiple local videos in one window and keep every clip in view while you compare timing, framing, and edits.

02

Drop files straight in

Drag and drop videos directly into your workspace so you can move from Finder to playback without setup overhead.

03

Fast playback control

Smooth playback, quick seeking, and progress scrubbing help you move through footage without breaking concentration.

04

Focus when needed

Switch into Focus Mode to isolate one video for a closer look, then return to your full comparison layout.

05

Track-level control

Choose audio tracks, switch subtitle tracks, or load external subtitle files without leaving the workspace.

06

Private by default

Your videos stay on your device, and Incognito Mode gives you a clean session that does not write workspace data to disk.

Local-first workflow

Made for real files on your Mac.

QuadPlay is designed around local video review. Bring in the footage you already have, arrange a workspace that matches the comparison you are doing, and stay in control of playback, tracks, and subtitles without routing your media through a cloud service.

Subscription

Simple plans, managed through Apple.

QuadPlay Pro is available as an auto-renewing 1-month or 1-year subscription. Pricing is shown on the in-app purchase screen at checkout.

Billing stays inside the Mac App Store flow.

Payment is charged to your Apple account when the purchase is confirmed. Renewal is processed within 24 hours before the current period ends, and the subscription extends by one more cycle after successful payment. To stop renewal, open Apple account subscription settings and cancel QuadPlay Pro at least 24 hours before the current subscription period ends.
