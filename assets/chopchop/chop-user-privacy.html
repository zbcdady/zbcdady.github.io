Last Updated: January 2026

Welcome to ChopChop Eats, operated by ChopChop Inc. (“ChopChop,” “we,” “us,” or “our”). ChopChop Eats is a marketplace that allows customers to browse, order, and receive homemade meals prepared by independent cooks (“Cooks”) through our mobile application and related services (collectively, the “Service”).


This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your information when you use ChopChop Eats.


By using the Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Service. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

We collect information you provide directly, including:

• Name, email address, phone number

• Delivery address and order details

• Payment information (processed securely by third-party payment providers)

• Messages, reviews, and feedback you submit

• Communications with customer support


Information Collected Automatically

When you use the Service, we may collect:

• Device and browser information

• IP address and approximate location

• App usage data, pages viewed, and interactions

• Log data and diagnostics for performance and security


Information from Third Parties


We may receive information from:

• Payment processors (to complete transactions)

• Delivery partners (to fulfill orders)

• Analytics and marketing providers

• Social media platforms if you choose to connect your account


2. How We Use Your Information


We use your information to:

• Create and manage your account

• Process order payments

• Facilitate delivery of meals

• Communicate with you about orders and support requests

• Improve app performance, features, and user experience

• Prevent fraud and ensure platform safety

• Send service-related and marketing communications (you may opt out of marketing)

3. How We Share Information


We may share information with:
