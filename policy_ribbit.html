Privacy Policy for Ribbit

Ribbit is operated by Orcava Inc. ("Orcava," "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect information when you use the Ribbit mobile application and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using Ribbit, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information: phone number, authentication credentials or verification status, Google Sign-In information, Sign in with Apple information, and the unique user ID associated with your account.
  • Third-party login details: for Google or Apple login, we receive only the information authorized by you and made available by those providers, which may include your name, email address, account identifier, or verified login status.
  • Profile information: child or user nickname, age or age range, selected avatar, instrument preference, and app profile settings.
  • Usage and practice data: practice session records, tempo/BPM settings, rhythm or training selections, session duration, streaks, badges, levels, progress statistics, and leaderboard-related scores or rankings.
  • Device and technical information: device type, operating system version, app version, language settings, basic diagnostic and performance data, and crash logs if enabled through platform tools or service providers.
  • Cloud sync and storage information: if you use features that sync across devices, we may store certain account, profile, and progress data on secure cloud services so your experience remains consistent across devices.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Authenticate users through phone number, Google, or Apple sign-in
  • Provide app functionality
  • Save progress and practice history
  • Sync account and profile information across devices
  • Display rankings, scores, and leaderboard features
  • Personalize the user experience
  • Improve app performance, reliability, and security
  • Respond to support requests and user inquiries
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Leaderboards and Social Features

Ribbit may use certain user information to support leaderboard and ranking features. This may include nickname or display name, avatar, and practice points, streaks, levels, or related performance metrics.

We only use the information reasonably necessary to operate these features. Users should avoid using sensitive personal information as nicknames or profile content.

4. Authentication Methods

Ribbit supports multiple sign-in methods, including phone number login, Google Sign-In, and Sign in with Apple.

When you use these login methods, your authentication is processed through the relevant provider or service. We may store account identifiers and basic account information needed to maintain your login and app profile. We do not receive your third-party account password.

Your use of Google Sign-In or Sign in with Apple may also be subject to those providers' own privacy policies and terms.

5. Children's Privacy

Ribbit is designed for children and families. We take privacy seriously, especially where children may use the app.

We collect only the information reasonably necessary to provide the Service, such as account login details, profile information, and practice progress. If a child uses the app, we expect that the account is created or supervised by a parent, guardian, or other authorized adult where required by applicable law.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information in a manner that does not comply with applicable law, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to investigate and, where appropriate, delete the information.

We do not sell personal information.

We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers that help us operate the app, such as cloud hosting, authentication, database, analytics, or infrastructure providers
  • With third-party login providers when required for authentication

  • If required by law, regulation, legal process, or government request
  • To protect the rights, safety, security, or property of users, Orcava Inc., or others

Any sharing is limited to what is reasonably necessary for the relevant purpose.

If you request deletion of your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information unless we are required or permitted by law to retain certain data.
